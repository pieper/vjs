VJS.slice. view

new VJS.slice.view(sliceCore)

modules/deprecated/slice/Slice.View.js, line 22

View class of slice component. Provides method to generate and interact all slice related visualization.

It includes the slice, the border of the slice, the slice normal, the intersection between the slice, the slice center of mass, the data bouding box, etc.

Doesn't hold any slice-related generic logic.
